# Melee Weapon

## Vastilok or Sarpa

![](<../../.gitbook/assets/image (34)>)

Vastilok can be replaced with Sarpa, but you’ll have an easier time with Vastilok

[**Shattering Impact**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Shattering_Impact) will remove the eidolon’s armor with just a couple of hits

If you’re using Outburst on your secondary weapon, make sure to add some Additional Combo Count Chance to your build

You can get Vastilok from other players: [https://warframe.market/items/vastilok?type=sell](https://warframe.market/items/vastilok?type=sell)
