# Primary Weapon

## Zenith

![Vigilante Armaments is deliberately rank 0](<../../.gitbook/assets/image (15)>)

{% hint style="info" %}
The **Zenith** is exclusive to the [**Daily Tribute**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Daily_Tribute) system. It will become available every 200 days, beginning at day 100, until chosen as the Milestone reward.

Additional blueprints can be bought from [**Cephalon Simaris**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Cephalon_Simaris) for [![](https://wiki.warframe.com/images/thumb/ReputationLarge.png/20px-ReputationLarge.png?ef954)](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Standing)100,000.
{% endhint %}
