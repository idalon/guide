# RIVEN GUIDE

## Primary - Zenith

#### **Dual-Stats**

**CC CD negative** ⇨ 2 Multishot Solo Build ⇨ Replace Critical Delay\
This Riven works, no specific grade, and it's really cheap

**MS CD negative** ⇨ 3 Multishot Solo/Duo Build\
Probably the best option in terms of flexibility

**MS DMG negative** ⇨ 3 Multishot Solo/Duo Build\
Lower Than MS CD

#### **Tri-Stats**

**MS DMG FR negative** ⇨ 2 Multishot Duo Build ⇨ Replace Fire Rate

**MS CD FR negative** ⇨ 2 Multishot Duo Build ⇨ Replace Fire Rate

100% Multishot is mandatory.\
These Rivens are often overkill and very expensive, but they allow you to run 10 fire rate w/ 2ms

## Melee - Vastilok

#### **Dual-Stats**

**AS CCC negative** ⇨ Classic Build\
Combo Count Chance allows you to quickly increase your combo and synergizes with [**Outburst**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Secondary_Outburst).\
Attack speed isn’t necessarily a required stat, since your build already has plenty of it, still ok

**IC CCC negative** ⇨ Classic Build\
The higher your initial combo, the faster you will reach x12

**IC AS negative** ⇨ Classic Build\
The higher your initial combo, the faster you will reach x12

#### **Tri-Stats**

**AS CCC IC negative** ⇨ Classic Build\
Clearly overkill and overpriced, but if you have it, play it.
