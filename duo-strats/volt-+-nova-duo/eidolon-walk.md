# Eidolon Walk

## Eidolon Walk = Limb Order

Sometimes a capshot is so fast that the Eidolon will play a **Kill Animation** (despite being captured, not killed), this leaves behind a **lingering hitbox** that will block your **Nova Portals.**

## Volt + Nova Duo Patterns

* **CL →** LRRRLL or RRRLLL
* **CR →** RLLLRR or LLLRRR
* **MID →** RLLLRR or LLLRRR
* **LONG →** RLRLRL or RRRLLL
