# Garry Volt Setup

#### Garry Water Preparation – Volt

Volt must:

1. Go to [LOC PIN CR](https://docs.google.com/document/d/17GKu52mvzQVJ5mcfDTcKP1q-mH6aMywsF3aNERU0E1w/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.1t3b3l60m4aa) and place the Med Tower (see placement)
2. Go to[ LONG](https://docs.google.com/document/d/17GKu52mvzQVJ5mcfDTcKP1q-mH6aMywsF3aNERU0E1w/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.1t3b3l60m4aa) and place his shields between 1:45 – 1:48
3. Go to[ LOC PIN CL](https://docs.google.com/document/d/17GKu52mvzQVJ5mcfDTcKP1q-mH6aMywsF3aNERU0E1w/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.1t3b3l60m4aa) and wait for Nova’s portal

Then:

* Return to Terry, pick up the Lures Nova placed on hold
* Perform the capshot

Back to the Shrine

* Place shields in front of the portals\
  ⚠️ Step back to prevent Lures from spawning inside portals or in the water.

Once the Lures explode:

* Retrieve the loot with Itzal 3
* Insert the Shard
* Move to the [Volt Water LOC PIN](https://docs.google.com/document/d/17GKu52mvzQVJ5mcfDTcKP1q-mH6aMywsF3aNERU0E1w/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.l5z4fk17ctur) and break Garry limbs
