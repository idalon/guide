# Volt / Nova Method

This is just one example of a working gameplay. You don’t have to follow it exactly. QC , lure management and water preparation can be adjusted

#### [hashtag](volt-nova-method.md#entering-the-plains) Entering the Plains

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### Volt

* Enters first, since he is the host
* Immediately starts his QC ( [Quick Chargearrow-up-right](https://docs.google.com/document/d/17GKu52mvzQVJ5mcfDTcKP1q-mH6aMywsF3aNERU0E1w/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.3r045j7u0d5e) )
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Nova

* Enters once Volt has charged 2 Vomvalysts on his first Lure
* Can then help Volt spawn the missing Vomvalysts
* Once Volt reaches the Vomvalysts Nova spawned, he can finish his QC
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

#### [hashtag](volt-nova-method.md#terry-first-limb) Terry – First Limb

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### Volt

* Heads to Terry to start breaking limbs
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Nova

* Goes to pick up 2 Lures, then positions herself in front of Terry to place them
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

Who breaks the limbs? → [BreakShield\&Limbarrow-up-right](https://docs.google.com/document/d/17GKu52mvzQVJ5mcfDTcKP1q-mH6aMywsF3aNERU0E1w/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.g6c6j6nbrazx)

<details>

<summary>Who breaks the limbs?</summary>

* Either Nova or Volt can do it
* Preferably Nova, since Volt must go get the Lures for the rest of the night

</details>

Aim to break the LLBT around 1:23.300 ( [see breakpoints + shrine cyclearrow-up-right](https://docs.google.com/document/d/17GKu52mvzQVJ5mcfDTcKP1q-mH6aMywsF3aNERU0E1w/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.lj6ay03mjg97) ) After that, prepare Garry Water
