# Garry Nova Setup

As soon as Terry goes down:

1. Place your Med Tower at LONG ( [see placementarrow-up-right](https://docs.google.com/document/d/17GKu52mvzQVJ5mcfDTcKP1q-mH6aMywsF3aNERU0E1w/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.fu6db3walg0w)).
2. Go to the Shrine.

Portal Placement

At the Shrine:

* Reverse camera orientation (default key H)\
  ![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/image (29)>)
* Stand inside the “shrine hole” (see video)

Place the portals in this order:

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### CL

Aim at Volt on the LOC PIN
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### MID

Aim at your own LOC PIN
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Reverse camera

Press H again to reverse camera
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### CR

Aim at Volt’s Med Tower
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### LONG

Go to LONG and place the last portal toward your Med Tower
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

Long Unstuck Setup – Nova

After taking the portal toward your Long tower:

* Leave your Warframe in front of the portal
* Go into Operator
* Jump + sling 2–3 times toward the shrine, staying over water
* Swap back to Warframe + Archwing
* Never fly over land or the setup breaks
* Land near the tip rock of the Shrine
* Cast Roar for VOLT
* Keep /unstuck pre-typed in chat

At the Shrine – VS + Propa Timing

When the host detonates the loot:

* Go Operator
* Use the Power Transfer timer to time your VS:
  * 13s for Garry Water

![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/image (19)>)

After the host inserts the shard:

Shoot the propas in this order (spam interact):

1. MID
2. CL
3. CR (just before or after your own shard inserts)

Then:

* /unstuck to Long
* Shoot the propa
* Turn around and break the limb
