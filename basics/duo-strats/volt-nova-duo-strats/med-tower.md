# Med-Tower

To get the med tower, you just need to obtain the MANTIS.

Then press Escape ➛ Orbiter ➛ Landing Craft ➛ Air Support ➛ Select Med-Tower

How to Place MedTower for Nova

1. AimGlide - Down
2. Press Antimatter keybind
3. Press Medtower keybind

![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/image (46)>)

How to Place MedTower for Volt

1. Take Shield
2. AimGlide simpler but not necessary
3. Press MedTower keybind
