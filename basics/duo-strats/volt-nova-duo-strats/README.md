# Volt / Nova DUO Strats

The fastest way to hunt Eidolons. It requires strong teamwork and a fair amount of time to learn, but it offers a different approach to Eidolon hunting, as solo and duo runs are played very differently.

Essential Requirements

1-7-7 AMP

* Raplak (1) → for breaking limbs
* Propa → AoE damage for waters
* Certus (7) → AMP critical chance

MedTower

Propa Mechanics (Water)

* Propa fires a single projectile
* The projectile explodes after a short delay
* The explosion hits the Eidolon's Ghost HitBox
