# Amp & Operator Arcanes

#### [hashtag](amp-and-operator-arcanes.md#amp-arcanes) AMP Arcanes

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
[**Eternal Eradicate**arrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Eternal_Eradicate) On Operator Ability:\
+60% Damage to Amps for 8s
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
[**Eternal Onslaught**arrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Eternal_Onslaught)\
+180% Critical Chance for 8 seconds after depleting Operator energy
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

#### [hashtag](amp-and-operator-arcanes.md#operator-arcanes) Operator Arcanes

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
[**Magus Repair**arrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Magus_Repair) or [**Magus Elevate**arrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Magus_Elevate)\
to heal your Warframe
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
[**Magus Melt**arrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Magus_Melt)\
up to 210% Heat Damage to your Amp, it aids in breaking Limbs with your Amp
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}
