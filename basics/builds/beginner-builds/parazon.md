# Parazon

![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/image (1)>)

* [**Runtime**arrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Runtime) is the best mod you can use, it can help you reach Terry as quickly as possible after your QC
* [**Live Wire**arrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Live_Wire) is good to have as it will stun nearby enemies while hacking
* [**Untraceable**arrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Untraceable) should be avoided for Nova Duos. If you play solo and want to use this mod make sure to modify your Cloaked AppearancetoSemi-CloakorGlow-Cloak in the Accessibility settings
