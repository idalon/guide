# Video Settings

Feel free to play as you like but here are a couple of tips

#### Volumetric Lighting OFF & Volumetric Fog Quality DISABLED

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (11).png" alt=""><figcaption><p><strong>Removes fog/mist</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

#### Particle Quality LOW & GPU Particle Quality DISABLED

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (8).png" alt=""><figcaption><p><strong>Particles are pretty distracting, disables the confetti</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

#### Distortions OFF

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (10).png" alt=""><figcaption><p><strong>Makes it easier to spot the Teralyst at a distance</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

#### Color Correction OFF

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (9).png" alt=""><figcaption><p><strong>This one is quite important to prevents flashbangs</strong></p></figcaption></figure>

#### Texture Memory HIGH

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (7).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="info" %}
For **Speedrunners** trying for the leaderboard we recommend turning off the rest of the settings to keep your as frame rate stable as possible. Alternatively upscaling with **DLSS** on **Performance** or **Ultra Performance** is decent enough and allows your game to stay "pretty"
{% endhint %}
