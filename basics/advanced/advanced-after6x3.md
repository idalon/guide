# Advanced (After6x3)

IDALON LINK: its a log analyzer, reviewing your hunt logs, letting you keep a record of your runs over time.

It’s imperative for new Hunters stuck at 6x3s without progress to shift focus toward Shrine Cycles and Breakpoints. These give you measurable, repeatable goals that actually reflect your improvement.

#### [Progression Goals in Solo](advanced-after6x3.md#progression-goals-in-solo)

[**Teralyst**](advanced-after6x3.md#teralyst)

Target the 1:23.300 Breakpoint.\
Example: if your Capture Time = 50 ms, then your Last Limb Break Time must be ≤ 1:23.250.

If you can’t manage it, it is highly recommended to progress in QuickCharge 2+2, Propa\&Raplak Timming100ms avg

You succeeded if your Shrine Time is 9.X s. If it's 10.X or 11 s, you missed

[**Gantulyst**](advanced-after6x3.md#gantulyst)

Target the 4:07.700.

At first you should hit this at least for CR and Mid. CL should be fairly easy too.

If you can’t manage it, it is highly recommended to progress in Sub 4.0s Waters, Propa\&Raplak Timming100ms avg, Lure Management
