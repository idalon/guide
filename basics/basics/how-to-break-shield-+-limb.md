# How To Break Shield + Limb

#### [hashtag](how-to-break-shield-+-limb.md#conditions) Conditions

* Have Raplak ( [127 / 177](../amp-builds-modules.md))
* [Mod](../amp-mods-thara.md) your AMP correctly
* [Magus Melt](../amp-and-operator-arcanes.md)
* [Volt Shieldsarrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Electric_Shield)
  * If you play Voltless, you will need the [Mandonelarrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Mandonel) archgun

#### [hashtag](how-to-break-shield-+-limb.md#gameplay) Gameplay

* Before shooting the Limb, place a [Shieldarrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Electric_Shield) between you and Eidolon\
  Voltless check → [Mandonel](../mandonel-voltless.md)
* You must stack Magus Melt x7 → [Void Sling faster](../how-to-void-sling-faster.md)
* Proc your Onslaught and Eradicate Arcanes
* Once the Limb is exposed, shoot it

Congratulations! You took the Shield and Limb
