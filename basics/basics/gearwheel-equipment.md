# Gearwheel equipment

## Archwing Launcher [hashtag](gearwheel-equipment.md#archwing-launcher)

* Requires [Archwing Launcherarrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Archwing_Launcher)
* [**Itzal** arrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Itzal) **is highly recommended** (obtained from Clan Dojo)
  * Cosmic Crush (3): Pulls vomvs and loot

## Ciphers [hashtag](gearwheel-equipment.md#ciphers)

* [100x cipher BParrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Cipher) from Clan Dojo
  * _36k Ferrite & 36k Nano Spores_
* Allows for instant lure hacking (Y)

## Squad Energy Restore (energy pads) [hashtag](gearwheel-equipment.md#squad-energy-restore-energy-pads)

* [100x energy pad BParrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Squad_Energy_Restore) from Clan Dojo
  * _5k Polymer Bundle & 30k Nano Spores_
* Pulses 100 Energy every 7.5s for 30s
* Great for emergency situations

## Air Support Charges: Med-Tower [hashtag](gearwheel-equipment.md#air-support-charges-med-tower)

* [100x air support BParrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Air_Support_Charges) from Clan Dojo
  * _12k Ferrite, 20k Salvage, 10 Morphics & 7k Plastids_
* It has collisions, so you can **detonate Propa** against it
* Greatly useful for **Nova stats**

## Loc-Pin [hashtag](gearwheel-equipment.md#loc-pin)

* [**BP** arrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Loc-Pin) can be obtained from Clan Dojo
* Permanent location marks

## Archgun (optional) [hashtag](gearwheel-equipment.md#archgun-optional)

* Any: Can equip [Primaryarrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Primary_Dexterity) and [Secondary Dexterityarrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Secondary_Dexterity) for the passive **melee combo duration**
  * Requires [**Archgun Arcane Adapter** arrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Archgun_Arcane_Adapter)
* [**Mandonel** arrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Mandonel) only: Buff Amp’s **Damage** and **Critical Chance**
  * Requires [**Archgun Deployer** arrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Archgun_Deployer)

## Necramech (optional) [hashtag](gearwheel-equipment.md#necramech-optional)

* Useful to keep Cetus Gate open between runs
* Can shoot undervomvs

## K-drive (optional) [hashtag](gearwheel-equipment.md#k-drive-optional)

* Can keep Cetus door open
* **Can fix many bugs,** basically, if you encounter a bug always try calling your K-drive and see if that fixes it (most times it will)
* Makes you immune to magnetic procs from the Eidolon (rarely if ever used for this)
* [Assembled K-Drivesarrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/K-Drive#Crafting) can equip the [Juice modarrow-up-right](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Juice) to regenerate energy

## Ancient Healer (highly optional) [hashtag](gearwheel-equipment.md#ancient-healer-highly-optional)

* Obtained from New Loka Syndicate
* Status immunity aura, includes knockdowns
