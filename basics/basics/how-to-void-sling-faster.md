# How to Void Sling faster

The best way to do this is to bind your “Jump” key to your mouse scroll wheel.

![](<../../.gitbook/assets/image (38)>)

Utility of Void Sling:

* Operator Energy Management: Your Operator regains 10 energy every time you Void Sling through an enemy (Eidolon).
* Madurai’s Sling Strength: Grants you a 40% Ability Strength bonus.
* Magus Melt Arcane: Adds 30% Heat Damage to your amp, stacking up to 7 times, essential for breaking the limb.
