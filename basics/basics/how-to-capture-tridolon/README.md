# How To Capture Tridolon

All Eidolons follow the same basic mechanic: you must first remove their shield with your AMP before you can damage their limbs.

#### Charging Eidolon Lures

Before destroying a limb, you need a charged Eidolon Lure. Lures can be found at Grineer camps, but they are uncharged when picked up.

To charge one, bring the lure near a Vomvalyst. When the Vomvalyst “latches” onto it, shoot the Vomvalyst to remove its outer shell. The lure will absorb it, gaining 1 charge.

#### Taking Synovias

Once your lure is charged, you can destroy one of the Eidolon’s synovias.\
When a synovia is destroyed, the Eidolon will fall for about 17 seconds\
At the start of the fall, it releases 4 magnetic waves, each applying magnetic status effects (energy drain)

Stay in Void Mode on your Operator to avoid taking damage on your Operator

Magnetic effects can be completely negated using an arcane included in all builds: Arcane Nullifier (+102% magnetic status resistance).

If a charged lure is not close to the Eidolon when it falls, the Eidolon will teleport to a random location

This cycle is repeated:

* 4 times for Teralyst
* 6 times for Gantulyst and Hydrolyst

#### Heal Phase

After all synovias are destroyed, the Eidolon enters a Heal Phase, lasting about 48 seconds. During this time, it summons Vomvalysts to heal itself. As it falls to the ground, it spawns 3–4 Vomvalysts:with intervals of 2–3 seconds

#### Capshot

When the Eidolon stands back up, you can perform a Capshot: this is where you deplete the final portion of its health bar, making it vulnerable anywhere on its body for the capture.\
As it rises, it will spawn additional Vomvalysts if needed, usually 4–6 in total.
