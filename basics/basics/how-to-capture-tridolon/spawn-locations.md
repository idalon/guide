# Spawn Locations

Gantulyst and Hydrolyst can spawn in four specific locations around the lake, and they can only be damaged from certain angles depending on their spawn point, as illustrated below.

![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/image (30)>)

The long spawn is a special case: the Eidolon appears partially underground, causing an additional delay when breaking its shield and limbs. This delay can be bypassed by using the Propa Det/Ground or Shraksun. We use the Zenith, whose passive allows us to destroy the limbs.

![](<../../../.gitbook/assets/image (31)>)

{% hint style="info" %}
Zenith can only be acquired from the Daily Tribute system (or additional copies from Cephalon Simaris), so it may take up to 100 days to get it. If you don’t have Zenith, Furis Incarnon is a viable alternative, though it’s less effective.
{% endhint %}
