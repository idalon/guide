# On Call Crew - OCC

This feature is incredibly useful for killing Vomvalysts as they spawn under the Eidolon, freeing you up to focus on other tasks.

* Unlock requirement: Command Rank 9 in the Railjack Intrinsic.

## Weapon choice for Crew

There are many good options for arming your On-Call Crew. Ideally, use a beam weapon like the Atomos, Kuva Nukor, Tenet Cycron, etc. However, even AoE weapons like the Ogris can be effective.
