# Wisp SOLO Strats

Wisp is currently one of the most consistent and meta frames for solo Eidolon hunts. She allows simple, fast, and highly consistent waters, thanks to her teleportation mechanics.

Builds

* [Wisparrow-up-right](https://docs.google.com/document/d/17GKu52mvzQVJ5mcfDTcKP1q-mH6aMywsF3aNERU0E1w/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.6wx01gm7cm72)
* [Zenith 3FR 3MSarrow-up-right](https://docs.google.com/document/d/17GKu52mvzQVJ5mcfDTcKP1q-mH6aMywsF3aNERU0E1w/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.ydokghha3599)
* Dual Toxocyst
* Vastilok

Essential Requirements

1-2-7 AMP

* Raplak (1) → for breaking limbs
* Shraksun (2) → AoE damage for waters
* Certus (7) → AMP critical chance

Shraksun Mechanics (Water)

* Shraksun fires a single projectile
* Short range: \~10 meters
* The projectile explodes after a short delay
* The explosion hits the Eidolon's Ghost HitBox

Wisp Water Mechanic (Water)

* Wisp teleports to one of her motes (Breach Surge)
* Each mote corresponds to a specific Garry or Harry spawn (https://docs.google.com/document/d/17GKu52mvzQVJ5mcfDTcKP1q-mH6aMywsF3aNERU0E1w/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.rrerswolpg24)
* Immediately follows up with Shraksun
* The projectile is fired directly into the Ghost HitBox
* The Shields destroyed instantly
* Returns to Warframe to finish the limb

Inputs Water & Keybinds

* Breach Surge → 3 default keybind
* Transference → 5 default keybind
* AMP secondary fire (Shraksun) → Mouse Wheel Click
* Melee attack → E default keybind

Timing and positioning are the key to constant
