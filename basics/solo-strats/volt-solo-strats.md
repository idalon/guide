# Volt SOLO Strats

Volt is currently one of the most consistent and meta frames for solo Eidolon hunts.

Builds

* Volt
* [Zenith 3FR 2MSarrow-up-right](https://docs.google.com/document/d/17GKu52mvzQVJ5mcfDTcKP1q-mH6aMywsF3aNERU0E1w/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.ydokghha3599)
* Dual Toxocyst
* MeleeLess

## Essential Requirements

1-2-7 AMP

1. Raplak (1) → for breaking limbs
2. Shraksun (2) → AoE damage for waters
3. Certus (7) → AMP critical chance

## Shraksun Mechanics (Water)

* Shraksun fires a single projectile
* Short range: \~10 meters
* The projectile explodes after a short delay
* The explosion hits the Eidolon's Ghost HitBox\*

## Inputs (Water) & Keybinds

* Melee attack → E (default keybind)
* Transference → 5 (default keybind)
* AMP secondary fire (Shraksun) → Mouse Wheel Click
* Melee attack → E (default keybind)

{% hint style="info" %}
Timing and positioning are the key to being consistent.
