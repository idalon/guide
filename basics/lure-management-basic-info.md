# Lure Management - Basic Info

![](<../.gitbook/assets/image (27)>)

Charging Lures is one of the most basic yet essential tasks in hunting. In total, you will need 8 charged lures to capture all three Eidolons: 2 for the Terralyst, 3 for the Gantulyst, and 3 for the Hydrolyst.

### Gathering lures

To start off, you need to gather lures. You can find them at any Grineer camp where they initially appear as enemies. Kill the lures, then approach and interact (default 'X') to open the hacking menu. You can either manually solve the puzzle to hack the lure or press 'Y' to automatically hack it (you need to have ciphers equipped in your gear wheel for this function). This will turn the lures into allies, ready to be charged and used to capture Eidolons.

### Charge your lures

You need to capture 3 Vomvalysts into each of your Lures to fully charge them. You can capture Vomvalyst into your Lure in one of two ways:

* Kill Vomvalysts that are tethered or nearby your lure, which will provide a charge (there's a visual cue of the Vomvalyst going into the Lure).
* Kill Vomvalysts in their Spectral Form using your Operator's Amp, then collect the Orb and bring it close to your lure to charge it.

To find Vomvalysts, you can:

* Fly around the map looking for them.
* Force spawn Vomvalysts at specific locations.
* Use the Vomvalysts that typically spawn under each Eidolon during its healing phase.

{% hint style="warning" %}
Make sure you always have at least one charged lure in close proximity to the Eidolon you're fighting. If you don't, the Eidolon will teleport (TP) after breaking one of its limbs, costing you nearly 30 seconds of time loss.
{% endhint %}

### Lure Management

Lure management is crucial, and you need to be aware of how many charged lures you have at any given point.

* Starting the Hunt: You need 1 lure charged before engaging with the Teralyst.
* Teralyst: You need 2 lures charged to capture the Teralyst. Ideally, have at least 3 lures charged so you can start with the Gantulyst without any delays.
* Gantulyst: You need 3 lures charged to capture the Gantulyst. Ideally, have at least 4 lures charged so you can continue with the Hydrolyst without any delays.
* Hydrolyst: Make sure you have 3 lures charged before capturing the Hydrolyst.
