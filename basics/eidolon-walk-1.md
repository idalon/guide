# Eidolon Walk

Walk pattern exists to avoid the risk of the Eidolon blocking the waters due to kill animations, which leave behind a lingering hitbox.

![](<../.gitbook/assets/image (32)>)

Patterns:

* CL = LRRRLL or RRRLLL
* CR = RLLLRR or LLLRRR
* MID = RLLLRR or LLLRRR
* LONG = RLRLRL or RRRLLL
