### Terry (Teralyst) – Fight

#### First Limb

* Do Terry’s first limb
* Immediately go pick up 2 lures

If you are too slow:

* Take 1 lure first
* Pick up the second one after the next limb

### Terry Down – Setup

Before finishing Terry:

* Make sure your lures are set to Hold Position
* Place your crewmate: [https://docs.google.com/document/d/17GKu52mvzQVJ5mcfDTcKP1q-mH6aMywsF3aNERU0E1w/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.lj9a4zg2ptu5](https://docs.google.com/document/d/17GKu52mvzQVJ5mcfDTcKP1q-mH6aMywsF3aNERU0E1w/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.lj9a4zg2ptu5)

### Garry Water Preparation

After the Terry down:

* Take at least 3 lures.
* Place your water setup motes (see loc pin position): [https://docs.google.com/document/d/17GKu52mvzQVJ5mcfDTcKP1q-mH6aMywsF3aNERU0E1w/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.rrerswolpg24](https://docs.google.com/document/d/17GKu52mvzQVJ5mcfDTcKP1q-mH6aMywsF3aNERU0E1w/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.rrerswolpg24)

Then:

* Perform the Terry capshot
* Return to the shrine
