# How To Break Shield + Limb

#### Conditions

* Have Raplak ([127 / 177](https://fenomeral.gitbook.io/eidolon-guide/builds/amp-builds-modules))
* [Mod](https://fenomeral.gitbook.io/eidolon-guide/builds/amp-mods-thara) your AMP correctly
* [Magus Melt](https://fenomeral.gitbook.io/eidolon-guide/builds/amp-and-operator-arcanes)
* [Volt Shields](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Electric_Shield)
  * If you play Voltless, you will need the [Mandonel](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Mandonel) archgun

#### Gameplay

* Before shooting the Limb, place a [Shield](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Electric_Shield) between you and Eidolon\
  Voltless check → [Mandonel](https://fenomeral.gitbook.io/eidolon-guide/advanced/mandonel-voltless)
* You must stack Magus Melt x7 → [Void Sling faster](https://fenomeral.gitbook.io/eidolon-guide/basics/how-to-void-sling-faster)
* Proc your Onslaught and Eradicate Arcanes
* Once the Limb is exposed, shoot it

Congratulations! You took the Shield and Limb
