# Wisp Method

This is just one example of a working gameplay.\
You don’t have to follow it exactly.\
QC and lure management can be adjusted

**Entering the Plains**

* Enter the Plains
* The easiest QC for Wisp is the one shown in the [link](https://youtu.be/Xce4AZBNfXQ)

**Terry (Teralyst) – Fight**

First Limb

* Do Terry’s first limb
* Immediately go pick up 2 lures

If you are too slow:

* Take 1 lure first
* Pick up the second one after the next limb

Limb References

* For limb timings and positions, refer to the [dedicated guide](https://docs.google.com/document/d/17GKu52mvzQVJ5mcfDTcKP1q-mH6aMywsF3aNERU0E1w/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.g6c6j6nbrazx)
* Note: [Mandonel ](https://docs.google.com/document/d/17GKu52mvzQVJ5mcfDTcKP1q-mH6aMywsF3aNERU0E1w/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.5dsldo4e5dtv)can be skipped for Terry, Raplak yellow crit (2.8%) won't break limbs but its a low stakes gamble at 197.2% Raplak CC

Mandonel for Terry is recommended, but quite complex to use [Link](https://youtu.be/XEftgKmor9A)

Terry Down – Setup\
Before finishing Terry

* Make sure your lures are set to Hold Position
* Place your [crewmate](https://docs.google.com/document/d/17GKu52mvzQVJ5mcfDTcKP1q-mH6aMywsF3aNERU0E1w/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.lj9a4zg2ptu5)

**Garry Water Preparation**

After the Terry down

* Take at least 3 lures.
* Place your water setup motes ([see loc pin position](https://docs.google.com/document/d/17GKu52mvzQVJ5mcfDTcKP1q-mH6aMywsF3aNERU0E1w/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.rrerswolpg24))

Then:

* Perform the Terry capshot
* Return to the shrine
