# Med-Tower

To get the [**Med-Tower**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Mantis#Med-Tower), you just need to obtain the [**Mantis**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Mantis) landing craft.

Then press Escape ➛ Orbiter ➛ Landing Craft ➛ [**Air Support**](https://wiki.warframe.com/w/Air_Support_Charges) ➛ Select Med-Tower

How to Place Med-Tower for Wisp

1. Melee out of your Archwing (looking up to avoid ground slamming)
2. Aim Glide looking down
3. Cast Will-O-Wisp (2 by default)
4. Med-Tower keybind ([<mark style="color:$success;">**Gear Hotkey key bindings**</mark>](../../basics/gearwheel-equipment.md))
