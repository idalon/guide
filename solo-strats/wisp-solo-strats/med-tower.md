# Med-Tower

To get the med tower, you just need to obtain the MANTIS.

Then press Escape ➛ Orbiter ➛ Landing Craft ➛ Air Support ➛ Select Med-Tower

### How to Place MedTower for Wisp

1. Aim Glide down (not essential, but simpler)
2. Will-O-Wisp keybind (2 by default)
3. Med Tower keybind
