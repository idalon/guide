# Advanced (After 6x3)

[**Idalon**](https://idalon.com/)**:** its a log analyzer, reviewing your hunt logs, letting you keep a record of your runs over time.

It’s imperative for new Hunters stuck at 6x3s without progress to shift focus toward Shrine Cycles and Breakpoints.\
These give you measurable, repeatable goals that actually reflect your improvement.

#### Progression Goals in Solo

**Teralyst**

Target the 1:23.300 Breakpoint.\
Example:\
if your Capture Time = 50 ms, then your Last Limb Break Time must be ≤ 1:23.250.

If you can’t manage it, it is highly recommended to progress in [<mark style="color:$success;">**QC 2+2**</mark>](../basics/quick-charge-2+2.md), [<mark style="color:$success;">**Propa & Raplak Timming 100ms avg**</mark>](../basics/propa-and-raplak-timing.md)

You succeeded if your Shrine Time is 9.X s.\
If it's 10.X or 11 s, you missed
