# Mandonel Voltless

Why do we use Mandonel?

Raplak CC 197.2%\
(2.8%)Yellow crit won't break Garry and Terry so even there you'd want to use Mandonel for consistency\
Harry shield+limb cannot be 1 tapped without Mandonel

Mandonel – Charged Shot Interesting effects:

x2 Damage\
+10% flat critical chance\
+50% critical damage\
Trail duration: \~8 seconds\
We recommend firing during the Eidolon's cry for maximum effectiveness.\
Usage: From the gear wheel\
Mandonel from a Necramech doesn't work

The trails are wide and easy to spot; use bright colors if necessary.
